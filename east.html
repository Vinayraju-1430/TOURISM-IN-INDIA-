East India

About East India

East India is known for its cultural diversity, lush landscapes, and historical significance. The region is home to the tea gardens of Assam, the temples of Odisha, and the vibrant city of Kolkata. Most popular tourist destinations for this region are Calcutta, former capital of India, Puri & Bhubaneshwar, Buddhist Circuit in Bihar, monasteries in Sikkim, Kanchenjunga Range of Mountains in Darjeeling, etc.

Top Tourist Destinations

Sundarbans

The Sundarbans is the largest mangrove forest in the world, home to the Royal Bengal Tiger and rich biodiversity.

Konark Sun Temple

The Konark Sun Temple in Odisha is an architectural marvel and a UNESCO World Heritage Site, renowned for its intricate carvings.

Kolkata

Kolkata, the "City of Joy," is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and colonial architecture.
